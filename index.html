Our Story

Started with one kitchen in 1982. Grown into a group.

The Bhatt Group traces back to 1982, when the family began cooking for weddings and functions around Palsana and Gangadhara — Gujarati, Punjabi and Chinese food made fresh, counter by counter. That kitchen became Bhatt Caterers, and over time, Bhatt Restaurant & Banquets, Bhatt Event Planner and Bhatt Tech & Support joined it.

Four different crafts today, but one family name on all of them — and one shared standard: do right by the people you're serving.
