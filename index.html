Our Story

Started with one kitchen. Grown into a group.

The Bhatt Group began with Bhatt Caterers — Gujarati, Punjabi and Chinese cooking made for weddings, functions and everyday celebrations around Bardoli and Gangadhara. What started as one family's kitchen has since grown into a small group of businesses, each carrying the same name and the same standard of care.

Today the group spans catering, dining, event planning and technology support — four different crafts, run with one shared belief: do right by the people you're serving.
